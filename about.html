About Us

Pengs Football T-Shirts

Pengs Football T-Shirts was founded by the visionary Thabang Moukangwe. He founded the business because he saw the lack of job opportunities in the community. That’s when he founded the business in 2020. This business cares about the community and sells affordable, high-quality t-shirts to everyone.

Mission

Our mission is to employ as many people from our community as possible and help feed families while selling the best high-quality t-shirts to football lovers around the world. We have hardworking employees who ensure quality every day.

Vision

We love the business and see it growing bigger soon. We plan to continue employing struggling members of the community and reduce unemployment. We will introduce more t-shirt designs and build a bigger factory to create more products.

Team Members
The Founder of Pengs Football T-Shirts

Thabang Moukangwe is the founder of Pengs Football T-Shirts. He was born on 2005/10/05 in Witbank, Mpumalanga. He had a vision and brought it to life. He is a big football fan and supports all the teams in the community.
