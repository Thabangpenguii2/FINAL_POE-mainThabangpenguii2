About Us

Peng Football T-Shirts

Peng Football T-Shirts was founded by visionary Thabang Moukangwe in 2020. The business was created to address unemployment in the community while providing affordable, high-quality football T-shirts for football lovers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create job opportunities for local communities while producing high-quality football T-shirts that are affordable and accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

We aim to grow into a leading football apparel brand, expand our product range, and build a larger production facility that supports more workers.

Founder

Thabang Moukangwe

Thabang Moukangwe was born in 2005 in Witbank, Mpumalanga. He is a passionate football fan who started this business to uplift his community and create opportunities for others.
